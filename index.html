Welcome to OverBaked!

  • Make as many pizzas within 60 seconds!

  • We can cook a pizza by placing
  • ingredients on the plate and
  • putting the plate into the oven

  • Plain pizza ingredients include:
  • bread, cheese, and sauce
  • Pepperoni pizza ingredients include:
  • bread, cheese, sauce, and pepperoni
  • To make tomato sauce:
  • place the tomato in the pot

  • Controls
  • "W" to move up
  • "A" to move left
  • "S" to move down
  • "D" to move right
  • "Space" to pick-up/place objects
